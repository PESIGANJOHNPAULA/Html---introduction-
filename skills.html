Time Management
Balancing time for studying, completing assignments, participating in estracurricular activities, and personal tasks. Good time management ensures that deadlines are met and helps prevent last-minute stress.
Critical Thinking
The ability analyze information objectively and make a reasoned judgement. It involves logical reasoning, problem-solving, and evaluating evidence.
Adaptability
The ability to adjust to new situations or changes quickly and efficiently. It involves flexibility, open-mindedness, and being proactive in learning new skills or methods.
